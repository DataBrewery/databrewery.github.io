Brewery is a framework for managing, analysing and mining data written in Ruby.
Goal is to provide functions and tools for:

* extraction, transformation and loading of data
* data quality auditing
* simple online analytical processing - "OLAP":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olap
